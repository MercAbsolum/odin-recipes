Taco Supreme

Ingredients

    Soft Tortillas
    1lb Beef
    Sour Cream
    Lettuce
    Tomatoes
    Shredded Cheese
    Hot Sauce

Steps

    Brown the beef on the stove
    Slice your tomatoes and lettuce
    Heat tortillas
    Place beef on tortillas
    Sprinkle tomatoes, lettuce, cheese and sour cream
    Add hot sauce to your taste
    Fold and cut in half
    EAT!
