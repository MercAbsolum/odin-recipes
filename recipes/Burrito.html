Burrito Surpreme

Ingredients

    2 flour tortillas
    Yellow rice
    1 pound of chicken
    Red and green peppers
    White onion
    Shredded cheese

Directions

    Heat skillet and add diced chicken
    Heat boiling water
    Cut vegetables
    Season chicken
    Stir rice and wait to fluff
    Add all ingrediants into tortilla
    Roll into burrito
